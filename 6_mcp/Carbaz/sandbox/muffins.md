# Blueberry Muffins Recipe

## Ingredients
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 cup 2% milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 cups frozen unsweetened blueberries

### Topping:
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Directions
1. PREP: 15 MIN. BAKE: 25 MIN. + COOLING
2. It will take you all of 15 minutes to prepare these easy blueberry muffins, which use frozen fruit and bake up in less than half an hour.
3. Preheat the oven to 3756F.
4. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and salt.
5. In a separate large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.
6. Then add the eggs, milk and vanilla extract, and mix everything well.
7. Stir the dry ingredients into the creamed ingredients, mixing just until everything is moistened.
8. Finally, fold in the frozen blueberries.
9. Fill 24 greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full of batter.
10. In a small bowl, combine the remaining sugar and nutmeg and sprinkle the blend over the muffins.
11. Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
12. Let the muffins cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing them to a wire rack.

Enjoy your delicious blueberry muffins!
